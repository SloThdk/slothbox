## Upstream projects we depend on

SlothBox stands on the shoulders of these:

- [libsodium](https://libsodium.gitbook.io/doc/) — audited cryptographic primitives
- [age](https://age-encryption.org/) — small, secure encryption library
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org), [Hono](https://hono.dev), [Drizzle ORM](https://orm.drizzle.team)
- [Postgres](https://www.postgresql.org), [MinIO](https://min.io), [Valkey](https://valkey.io), [NATS](https://nats.io)
- [Caddy](https://caddyserver.com), [Grafana](https://grafana.com), [Prometheus](https://prometheus.io), [Loki](https://grafana.com/oss/loki/)
- [.NET](https://dotnet.microsoft.com), [Go](https://go.dev), [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org)
- The [FreeTSA.org](https://freetsa.org) project for free RFC 3161 timestamping

If you maintain or contribute to one of these and want recognition above what
the standard release-notes credits give, get in touch.
